How to Play

Controls

  • ✓ Movement: Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • ✓ Attack: Automatically attacks nearby enemies
  • ✓ Special Ability: Press SPACE to use your special ability
  • ✓ Collect Items: Walk over items to collect them

Game Objective

Your goal is to survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters. Each minute you survive, the game becomes more challenging with stronger and more numerous enemies.

Collect experience gems to level up and choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones. Find special items to enhance your character's stats and survivability.

Game Features

Diverse Characters

Choose from multiple unique characters, each with their own special abilities and playstyles. From tank warriors to ranged mages, find the perfect match for your gameplay preference.

Endless Progression

Level up your character and unlock powerful upgrades. Each run provides opportunities to grow stronger with a vast skill tree that offers different build paths and strategies.

Monster Variety

Face dozens of different monster types with unique behaviors and attack patterns. From basic slimes to massive boss creatures, the enemy diversity keeps every game exciting.

Treasure & Power-ups

Discover rare items and power-ups that dramatically change your gameplay. Find synergistic combinations that create devastating effects against the monster hordes.

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game inspired by the roguelike genre. In this thrilling adventure, you'll face off against endless waves of monsters, each more challenging than the last.

The game features a unique auto-attack system where your character automatically targets nearby enemies, allowing you to focus on movement and positioning. As you defeat monsters, you'll collect experience points to level up and choose from a variety of abilities and upgrades.

With its pixelated art style and fast-paced gameplay, Monster Survivors offers a nostalgic yet fresh gaming experience. The game's simple controls make it accessible to newcomers, while the depth of its progression system provides plenty of strategic options for experienced players.

How long can you survive? Challenge yourself to beat your high score and unlock all the game's secrets!
